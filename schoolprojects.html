School Project: Unreal Engine Map

In this exercise, you will practice the skills you just learned in Unreal. The project is to reproduce the following map. To get the full points you must reproduce it in its entirety, including decorating the rooms as labeled. You may use any assets you wish, but don't be too picky if you can't find what you want. You can find assets in the marketplace, Quixel Bridge, and at turbosquid.com.

  • Entire map reproduced with appropriate dimensions (one square is the size of about 3 feet)
  • Every room must be accessible via a doorway
  • Every room is decorated appropriately, for example, toilets in the head.
  • Must be made in first-person mode
  • Must submit screenshots or YouTube video of the map (video must be 3 minutes or less)
